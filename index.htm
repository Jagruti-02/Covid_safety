    PRECAUTIONARY MEASURES

    • Wash hands often

      Cover all surfaces of your hands. Avoid touching your eyes, nose, and mouth with unwashed hands.

    • Wear mask

      Consider wearing a mask in crowded outdoor settings and for activities with close contact with others who are not fully vaccinated.

    • Get Vaccinated

      Authorized COVID-19 vaccines can help protect you from COVID-19. You should get a COVID-19 vaccine when it is available to you.

    • Maintain social distance

      Stay at least 6 feet (about 2 arm lengths) from other people. Keeping distance from others is especially important for people who are at higher risk of getting very sick.

    • Sanitize and disinfect

      Clean high touch surfaces daily and use a hand sanitizer that contains at least 60% alcohol.

    • Cover coughs and sneezes

      Always cover your mouth and nose with a tissue when you cough or sneeze, or use the inside of your elbow and do not spit.
